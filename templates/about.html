What is the Universal Knowledge Graph?

The Universal Knowledge Graph (UKG) is an advanced computational intelligence system designed for dynamic multi-perspective knowledge synthesis and expert simulation across multiple cognitive layers. It employs a sophisticated 10-layer architecture that enables complex reasoning, probabilistic knowledge modeling, and recursive self-awareness mechanisms.

At its core, the UKG organizes knowledge across 13 dimensions or "axes" to create a comprehensive representation of expertise, regulatory frameworks, methodologies, and contextual understanding. This structure enables the system to approach problems from multiple perspectives, simulating the expertise of diverse domain specialists.

The 10-Layer Architecture

Layer 1

Knowledge Base

Foundation layer providing access to structured and unstructured knowledge across all 13 axes of the knowledge framework.

Layer 2

Quad Persona Engine

Enables multi-perspective analysis through the simulation of four distinct expert personas with specialized knowledge domains.

Layer 3

Research Simulation

Simulates research processes, methodology applications, and evidence-based reasoning for thorough exploration.

Layer 4

POV Engine

Manages perspective-taking and contextual understanding to generate insights from multiple viewpoints simultaneously.

Layer 5

Integration Layer

Synthesizes outputs from lower layers, resolving conflicts and ensuring coherence across different knowledge domains.

Layer 6

Memory Systems

Hierarchical memory architecture with long-term knowledge storage and contextual awareness for continuous learning.

Layer 7

Simulated AGI System

Provides goal-oriented reasoning, planning, and dynamic problem-solving capabilities for complex scenarios.

Layer 8

Quantum Computer Simulation

Applies quantum computing principles to knowledge processing for probabilistic reasoning and multi-dimensional analysis.

Layer 9

Recursive AGI Core

Enables self-inspection, recursion, and improvement through analysis of its own reasoning processes.

Layer 10

Self-Awareness Engine

Provides system monitoring, error detection, and uncertainty quantification across all processing layers.
